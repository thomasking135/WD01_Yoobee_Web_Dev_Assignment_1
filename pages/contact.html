About the owner

Thomas King is the owner of Salty's. He's been in love with fish and chips since he was the height of a grasshopper He is passionate about taking this humble Kiwi love to soaring new heights. He has been managing the chippery since 2017 and takes great pride in the fabulously fresh fare that he serves to his faithful flock. But don't take his word for it.
